2020Text-1

  1. A of B

    核心词是A

    例外 如果A是数词 核心词还是B

    A lot of books

    A number of books

    An great amount of oil

  2. Dreadful adj. 糟糕透顶的；讨厌的；令人不快的

  3. Handicap n. 残疾 n. 不利条件 v. 损坏

  4. 状语 副词adv.

    Adv. 修饰动词或者形容词

    I play soccer well

    关系连词+分句，主谓宾

    主谓宾, 关系连词+分句

    When I am happy, I can play soccer.

    当分句主语和主句主语一样的时候，可以省略分句的主语，如果分句主语有be, be动词也可以省略。

    When written, this book is new.

  5. Glow vi. 发光

    ​ v. 脸上发光（害羞 /高兴）

    glowing adj. 热烈赞扬的；热情洋溢的

  6. Parallel n.adj. 平行的 平行线

    Parallel parking

    Unparalleled adj. 无比的；无双的；空前的

  7. 非谓语

    主谓宾

    没有加逗号

    I like to use computers of Huawei having some software.

    I like to use computers bought by my dad.

    有逗号

    I like to do exercises, making me happy.

    I like to read books, writing some notes.

    I like to use computers, bought by my dad.

    writing some notes, I like to read books

  8. 定语从句 adj.

    修饰名词

    限定性定语从句，没有逗号

    I have an apple that/which/who is red.

    I have a computer whose software is good.

    I like this book you read.

    当先行词在定语从句中做宾语的时候，

    关系代词可以省略.

    非限定性定语从句 有逗号 只能用which

    I like to read books, which is useful for me.

    I like to read books, which are expensive.

    带介词定语从句

    I like this table on which there is a book.

    I like this book through which I can learn many things.

    I like this school where I can learn many things.

    I like this week when I am happy.

    定语从句和状语从句区别

    Where when

  9. 形式主语

    It is adj (for sb) to do sth

    It is important to learn math

    It is useful that I learn math.

    As 关系连词

    引导状语从句

    因为，当

    正如

    As someone pointed out,

    Just as 介词 正如 作为

    As a person, I should learn things.

    I regard you as a student.

    I regard/think of/view you as imp

  10. Retreat v.n. 撤退

    Prove 系动词（be）

    Prove adj.

    Become/taste/sound/look/smell/feel +adj.

  11. Find A B

    Make A(n.) B(n. v. 介词，adj.)

  12. Predominance n. 优势 主导

    Dominate v. 主导

  13. at a loss over 困惑 不知所措

    A is at a loss over B

  14. fade vi. 消退 退却

    fadeaway n. 后仰跳投

  15. shrink vi.消退 消散

    shrunk v.

    shrink n. 心理医生

  16. vanish vi. 消失 突然不见

    domestic adj. 国内的 家庭的 驯养的（wild）

  17. on the ropes n. 濒于失败；完全无力回击；处于困境

  18. casualty n. 伤亡人员；受害者

  19. take sth for granted 把sth当成理所应当的

    宾语从句

    主谓宾

    I like this apple.

    I believe (that) you like this apple.

    Bring A

  20. inquire v. 咨询 询问 查询; 打听

    enquire

    insure v. 上保险

    insurance n. 保险

    ensure v. 确保

  21. attribute V. 归因于

    attribute A to B

    I have some fruits such as apples and watermelons

    i have some such fruits as apples and watermelons

  22. yield vt. 产出

    ​ n. 产品 产量

    yield to vi. 屈服于

  23. impetus n. 推动力; 促进因素

  24. manifest vt.显现 表明

  25. shift v.转换

  26. pave the way 奠定基础

  27. contribute to 促使 导致

  30. As…as
    Your books are as useful in many fields as my books.
    第一个as没有意思，
    中间部分和前面的句子连在一起
    第二个as理解成“正如”
    You play soccer as beautifully as he does.

    I can play soccer as well as he does.

  39. Mediocrity n. 平庸，平常
    Mediocre adj. 平庸的
  40. Utopia n.乌托邦
    Those apples

    一般默认为人
  41. Ignorant adj. 无知的，（对某事物）不了解的
    Be ignorant of sth
  42. Savage n. 野蛮人; 残暴的人
                         Adj. 野蛮的; 残暴的
  43. Comprehend understand
    Comprehension n. understanding
    Comprehensive adj. 综合的
    However pretty you are, you still need to learn math.
  44. Used to do 过去做
    Be/get used to doing 习惯做
    Be used to do 被用来做…

    Hardly rarely seldom scarcely not
